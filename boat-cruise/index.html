The Experience

A Private Boat, Just for Your Crew

Board the classic wooden vessel Marie L right from Portland's Old Port and spend the afternoon or evening exploring the 200+ islands of Casco Bay. No strangers. No shared tours. The entire boat is yours.

Your captain knows every cove, hidden beach, and island anchorage in the bay. Choose a sightseeing cruise past Maine's iconic lighthouses, catch the golden hour on a sunset charter, celebrate with bottomless mimosas at brunch, or take in New England's fall colors from the water.

This is the best bachelorette activity in Maine. Hands down.

Common Questions

Bachelorette Boat Cruise FAQ

How many people can join the bachelorette boat cruise?

Our large group charter accommodates up to 16 guests — perfect for a full bachelorette party crew.

What is included in the charter?

Every charter includes the vessel Marie L, a licensed captain, and your choice of experience (mimosa brunch, sunset charter, sightseeing, or fall foliage). Drinks are included; catering from Chaval and The Ugly Duckling can be added.

When should I book?

Summer and fall weekends fill up quickly — we recommend booking 4–6 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date. Spring and weekday dates tend to have more availability.

Where does the boat depart from?

The charter departs from Portland's Old Port waterfront at 320 Fore Street — walkable from downtown hotels, restaurants, and bars.

Can we add catering to our charter?

Yes — catering from Portland's acclaimed Chaval and The Ugly Duckling is available on every charter. Select your menu during the booking process or up to 72 hours before departure. You're also welcome to bring your own food and non-alcoholic beverages.

Who is catering the food on the charters?

The catering comes from Ilma Lopez and Damian Sansonetti — the James Beard Award-nominated chefs behind Chaval and The Ugly Duckling, two of Portland's most celebrated restaurants. Their menus feature Chaval-inspired tapas, a buttery brioche lobster roll, artisan pastries, charcuterie, and premium caviar service.

What if the weather is bad?

Maine weather is unpredictable, but we sail in most conditions. If weather requires a cancellation, we'll work with you to reschedule at no additional charge.
