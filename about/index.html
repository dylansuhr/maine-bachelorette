About

Maine Bachelorette

A planning and booking resource for brides discovering Portland, Maine.

Maine Bachelorette is a guide and booking resource for brides planning bachelorette weekends in Portland, Maine. This site exists because Portland is one of the best bachelorette destinations on the East Coast — and not enough groups know it yet.

The restaurant scene punches well above the city's size. The coast is legitimately beautiful. A private boat charter on Casco Bay with an unlimited open bar and James Beard–nominated catering is a rare thing to find anywhere, let alone two hours from Boston.

The guides here are built from real experience with Portland — what's actually worth doing, where to stay, how to get here, and how to put together a weekend your group will talk about for years.

Why Portland, Maine

The Best-Kept Bachelorette Secret on the East Coast

Nashville is saturated. Scottsdale is expensive and hot. Portland is a 2-hour drive from Boston — a compact, walkable city with a world-class food scene and a coastline that looks like it belongs in another country.

The private boat charter experience here — unlimited open bar, a classic wooden vessel, 200+ islands — is something most cities can't match.
