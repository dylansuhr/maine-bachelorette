About Maine Bachelorette

Maine Bachelorette is a guide and booking resource for brides planning bachelorette weekends in Portland, Maine. We built this site because Portland is genuinely one of the best bachelorette destinations on the East Coast — and not enough groups know it yet.

The restaurant scene punches well above the city's size. The coast is legitimately beautiful. A private boat charter on Casco Bay with an open bar and James Beard–nominated catering is a rare thing to find anywhere, let alone two hours from Boston.

Our job is to make it easy to find, plan, and book. The guides on this site are built from real experience with Portland — what's actually worth doing, where to stay, how to get here, and how to put together a weekend your group will talk about for years.

What We Cover

  • Private bachelorette boat cruises on Casco Bay — the centerpiece of every great Maine bachelorette weekend
  • Maine bachelorette activities — from sunrise kayaking to oysters at Eventide to rooftop cocktails
  • Portland Maine weekend guide — where to stay, eat, drink, and what to do across a full 3-day itinerary

Why Portland, Maine

Nashville is saturated. Scottsdale is expensive and hot. Portland, Maine is the bachelorette destination that most groups from Boston, New York, and Providence haven't discovered yet — which is exactly what makes it so good right now.

It's a 2-hour drive from Boston, a compact walkable city with a world-class food scene, and a coastline that looks like it belongs in another country. The private boat charter experience here — an unlimited open bar, a classic wooden vessel, 200+ islands — is something you genuinely can't replicate in most cities.

We think it's the best-kept bachelorette secret on the East Coast. This site exists to share it.
